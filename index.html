Dare: Be bold, go vegan
for your health,
our environment, and the animals

Vegans like Patrik Babaumian believe the true secret to strength is rooted in compassion. That's because the strong protect the innocent, especially animals who suffer everyday in the agriculture, fashion, research, and entertainment industries.

If you were in their place, you'd want someone to alleviate your suffering and advocate on your behalf.
